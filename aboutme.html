Travelling

Why I love to travel?

It's the best way to make yourself uncomfortable and get out of your comfort zone. Like when I came to Canada, just playing football was complicated because I could understand some English but expressing myself was complicated and I had to get out of my comfort zone so I can learn from every interaction I had. So to me travelling is a bit the same but I used to be someone that travelled with friends a couple a years ago but then I realised that solo travelling is way better because:

  • You are forced to integrate into the culture and learn the language, foods, lifestyles and etc
  • Make new connections with other solo travellers
  • You have more of a freedom because you are not organising with travelling partner or group
  • Can stay for however long you want
  • And much more!
